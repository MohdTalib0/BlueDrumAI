## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** (already in use)
- Clean, modern
- Excellent readability
- Professional appearance

### Logo Text Options

**Option 1: Bold & Modern**
```
BLUE DRUM AI
```
- All caps
- Bold weight
- Tracking: -0.08em
- Size: 24-32px

**Option 2: Balanced**
```
Blue Drum AI
```
- Title case
- Semi-bold weight
- Natural tracking

**Option 3: Minimalist**
```
bluedrum.ai
```
- Lowercase
- Medium weight
- Modern, tech-forward

## Logo Specifications

### Sizes Needed

1. **Favicon:** 32x32px, 64x64px
2. **App Icon:** 512x512px, 1024x1024px
3. **Social Media:**
   - Twitter: 400x400px
   - LinkedIn: 300x300px
   - Facebook: 180x180px
4. **Website Header:** 200x60px (horizontal)
5. **Product Hunt:** 240x240px
6. **Email Signature:** 200x60px

### File Formats

- **SVG** - Vector (primary)
- **PNG** - High-res (transparent background)
- **JPG** - Fallback (white background)
- **ICO** - Favicon

## Design Principles

### Do's ✅
- Keep it simple and recognizable
- Ensure scalability (works at small sizes)
- Use brand colors consistently
- Maintain professional appearance
- Test on dark/light backgrounds

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use too many colors
- Avoid complex details
- Don't make it too literal
- Avoid trendy fonts that date quickly
- Don't compromise readability

---

## Recommended Final Concept

**Recommended: Concept 1 (Shield with Drum)**

**Rationale:**
- Combines brand name (drum) with core value (protection)
- Professional and trustworthy appearance
- Scalable and recognizable
- Works across all use cases
- Unique but not overly complex

**Implementation:**
- Shield shape with rounded corners
- Stylized drum inside (can be simplified to circles/lines)
- Blue gradient (#3b82f6 to #1e40af)
- "Blue Drum AI" text below or beside
- Inter font, semi-bold
